JESSE DAVID DYER
1993 - 2022
Jesse's family is sorry to say that Jesse passed away the week of November 7th.
We do not know why Jesse died. He seemed happy and healthy when he last spoke to his father on the 7th for their daily work check-in. That was the last time he was heard from.
Jesse was found dead in his Boise apartment on November 12th. An autopsy by the Boise coroner was inconclusive.
He is survived by his sisters, Sarah and Mary, and his parents, Jeff and Gayle.
We plan to hold a memorial celebration for Jesse at his childhood home in Mountain View in early spring 2023. If you knew Jesse and would like to stay in touch with his family, we would love to hear from you. Please send us a note at hello@jessedaviddyer.org.
